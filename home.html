Mary Jane Nacorda – Crafting Digital Experiences

Hi there! I'm Mary Jane Nacorda, an aspiring web designer currently pursuing a BS in Information Technology at Cebu Eastern College Incorporated. My passion lies in creating visually stunning and user-friendly websites that bring ideas to life. In this blog, I’ll be sharing my journey as a web designer—my inspirations, the challenges I face, and the lessons I learn along the way. From front-end development to UX/UI principles, I'll document my growth, hoping to inspire others who dream of making a mark in the digital world.
